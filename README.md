"When will my ex come back? When will I get married or get a job?" 🤔 We've all seen those reels filling up our feed, right? Well, what if I told you we can get some answers, and not just vague ones, but personalized insights into your future? 🌌

At the recent LevelSupermind Hackathon, my friend and I coded the whole night to create KarmaKoder—a platform that blends astrology with modern tech, giving you personalized predictions.

✨ What can KarmaKoder do?
Just upload your:
🔗 Name
📅 Birthdate, time, and location
🌍 Choose your language (English, Hindi, Marathi)

And get:
🔮 Your Horoscope for the day/month/year
💎 Gemstone recommendations
🏠 Kundali (12 houses) analysis
🧘‍♂️ Meditation and workout plans
🛕 Suggested poojas you should perform
📈 Future triggers—what's next in your life
💬 Chatbot to answer all astrology-related questions
🖐️ Palm reading—just upload a hand image, and we'll reveal your predictions!

We’ve created KarmaKoder to give you answers to those big questions and more—whether you're curious about relationships, career, or just exploring your destiny.
